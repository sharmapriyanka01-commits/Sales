# Test Scenarios

Use these prompts to validate the skill behavior.

## Scenario 1: Enterprise device refresh
Prompt:
"Create a one-page battle card for the latest iPhone targeting enterprise IT buyers in North America comparing against Samsung Galaxy S-series and Google Pixel. Include objections and discovery questions."

Expected checks:
- Includes all 8 template sections.
- Uses business outcomes (security, lifecycle cost, deployment velocity).
- Includes 5+ discovery questions and 4+ objections/rebuttals.

## Scenario 2: SMB retail motion
Prompt:
"Draft a call-prep brief for SMB retail owners considering iPhone vs Android options. Keep it concise and practical for a 15-minute call."

Expected checks:
- Uses call-prep brief variant.
- Maintains evidence-based and non-inflammatory competitor framing.
- Ends with a concrete next-step CTA.
