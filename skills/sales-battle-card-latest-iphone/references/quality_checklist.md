# Quality Checklist for Latest iPhone Battle Card

## Accuracy and evidence
- Verify every technical claim is sourced from approved/public information.
- Remove or label rumors and unverified launch speculation.
- Ensure competitor claims are current to the same timeframe.

## Messaging quality
- Keep bullets short and usable in live conversation.
- Tie each feature to a business outcome.
- Keep tone factual and non-inflammatory toward competitors.

## Sales usability
- Include at least 5 discovery questions.
- Include at least 4 objections with rebuttals and proof points.
- Provide a clear next-step call to action.

## Segment fit
- Confirm examples and language match the target segment (enterprise, SMB, retail, carrier).
- Localize availability/compliance statements for region if relevant.

## Final polish
- Remove duplicate points.
- Keep final output skimmable (headers, tables, bullets).
- Include an assumptions section whenever data gaps remain.
